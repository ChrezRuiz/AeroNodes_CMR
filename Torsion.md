the stress effect on a shaft due to two opposite rotating forces on each end

### General Equation
$$\frac{T}{J}=\frac{G\theta}{L}$$ where: 
- $T=\text{Torque}$
- $J=\text{Second Polar Moment}$
- $G=\text{Modulus of Rigidity}$
- $L=\text{Length}$
